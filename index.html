Projects & publications

Dissertation · May 2026 · 73%

AI chatbots versus human therapists

A mixed-methods study (N = 101) comparing AI chatbots and human therapists on emotional connection, effectiveness, trust, disclosure, accessibility and preference. Human therapists rated significantly higher across all dimensions (all p < .001; dz = −1.16 to −2.63), with AI valued for affordability and access but seen as supplementary.

paired & one-sample t-tests · Framework Analysis · anonymised, GDPR-compliant survey

Systematic review · Dec 2025 · 85%

AI-enabled movement approaches for depression & anxiety

An independent PRISMA 2020 review with an a-priori PRISMA-P protocol. I searched five databases (PsycINFO, PubMed, Scopus, Web of Science, SPORTDiscus), screened 688 records, included 7 studies, appraised quality with RoB-2 and JBI, and synthesised findings to PRISMA standards. Manuscript in preparation.

PRISMA 2020 / PRISMA-P · RoB-2 · JBI critical appraisal

R analysis · 2026

Actigraphy sleep analysis in R

Built an R pipeline that processes epoch-level wrist-actigraphy data, classifies sleep versus wake, and derives standard sleep metrics — total sleep time, wake after sleep onset, sleep onset latency and sleep efficiency — for each night, with an actogram and nightly-metrics visualisations. Self-directed project on a simulated 7-day dataset; the same approach applies to real device data, typically processed with the GGIR package.

sleep/wake classification · TST, WASO, sleep efficiency · actogram · base R

R analysis · 2026

Recognition-memory task scoring in R

Wrote an R script that scores a recognition (old/new) memory task, classifying each trial (hit, miss, false alarm, correct rejection) and computing signal detection theory metrics — d′ (sensitivity) and criterion (bias) — alongside accuracy and reaction time, per participant, with summary visualisations. Demonstration on a simulated dataset; builds on the signal detection analysis I carried out in my RA role with Dr Balgova.

signal detection theory · d′, criterion · reaction-time analysis · base R
